milestones

some snapshots of what I’ve been up to —exhibitions, installations, and lots of creative problem solving. Each step’s been a chance to learn, grow, and make something I’m proud of.

moving to melbourne

In February 2022, I moved to Melbourne to start my studies at the University of Melbourne. Moving across the world was both scary and exciting, but it’s a decision I’m incredibly grateful for. It’s given me the chance to travel, see stunning parts of this side of the world, and immerse myself in cultures so different from my own.

working w/ australia post

I was contacted by the Business Systems Manager (SAP Development) of Australia post November 2023, and asked to present a talk on UI and UX principles to his team of 20 developers. As part of the session, I also conducted a detailed review of one of their applications, offering insights to improve functionality and user experience.

exhibitions

In 2024, five of my works got exhibited at two different exhibitions: the Victoria College of Art's End of Year Exhibition, and at University of Melbourne's designX 2024.

In addition to this, one of my posters designs were picked to be hung up permanently at the Elizabeth Murdoch Building on the University of Melbourne’s Southbank campus.

graduating

I graduated from the University of Melbourne December 2024 with a bachelor of Design, double majoring in User Experience Design and Graphic Design, with a Weighted Average Mark of 79.7 —Graduating in Second Class Honours Division A.
